Place each letter exactly once in every row and column — the rest of that row/column stays blank. Every puzzle is solvable by logic alone, no guessing.

The rules

  • Each letter appears exactly once in every row and column. The unused cells in each row/column stay blank.
  • An outside letter is the first non-blank letter you'd hit scanning in from that side.
  • Some sides are left blank — that clue just wasn't needed to solve the puzzle by logic alone. Every puzzle is still solvable with only the clues shown.

Controls

  • Tap a square to cycle it: blank ✗ → A → B → C (→ D on Hard) → empty.
  • Press & hold a square to mark it ? when you're unsure. Tap it once to clear the question mark.
  • Press & drag to mark a run of squares as blank ✗.
