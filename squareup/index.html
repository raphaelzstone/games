Every day's shape is exactly 25 squares — cut from a 5×5 square, with one piece moved. Find the one way to split it back into two pieces that reform the square (pieces may rotate and/or flip).

  • Tap a cell to toggle which piece it belongs to — the two piece colors update live.
  • There's exactly one correct split. Hit Check once you think you've found it.
  • One puzzle a day. Your score is your time — lower is better. Stuck? Reveal costs a 30-second penalty.
