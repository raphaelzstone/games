One hidden 3-letter word fills the blank squares in every row, forming four 6-letter words reading across.

  • The blanks step down one square each row — a staircase. The same three letters go in each row.
  • Type your 3-letter guess; the rows fill in live so you can see the words take shape.
  • Each puzzle is 1:30 on the clock and worth up to 500 points: full value in the first 0:15, sliding down to a 300-point floor by 1:15, then flat for the last 0:15. Run out the clock without solving it and that puzzle scores 0. Three puzzles, 1500 points max — higher is better.
