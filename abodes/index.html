Pitch a tent next to every tree — one tent per tree, joined up, down, left or right. Every puzzle is solvable by logic alone, no guessing.

The rules

  • As many tents as trees, paired one-to-one with a neighbouring tree.
  • No two tents touch — not even at a corner.
  • The numbers along the right and bottom edges tell you how many tents belong in that row or column — they're your main clues.

A tree greys out once it's certainly matched to a tent — a settled tree you can stop worrying about. A tent shared between two possible trees leaves both until it's resolved.

Controls

  • Tap a square to select it.
  • With a square selected, tap ✗, Tent, or ? below the board to set it. Tapping the same button again clears it back to empty.
  • Press & drag to mark a run of squares as grass.
  • Tap an edge number to fill the rest of that row/column with grass.
