Pitch a tent next to every tree — one tent per tree, joined up, down, left or right. Every puzzle is solvable by logic alone, no guessing.

The rules

  • As many tents as trees, paired one-to-one with a neighbouring tree.
  • No two tents touch — not even at a corner.
  • Each number counts the tents in that row or column.

Controls

  • Tap a square to cycle it: grass ✗ → tent → empty. (One tap marks grass, a second makes it a tent — no rush.)
  • Press & drag to mark a run of squares as grass.
  • Tap a number to fill the rest of that row/column with grass.
